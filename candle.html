Candle Making Workshop

The Candle Making Workshop was a hands-on creative session that introduced participants to the fascinating process of designing and crafting their own candles.

Attendees explored various techniques such as basic wax pouring, layering, coloring, and scent infusion. The workshop emphasized sustainable practices using eco-friendly wax and reusable molds.

Workshop Instructor

Rashi Mehta

A professional candle artist and aromatherapist from Jaipur, Rashi combines traditional wax techniques with modern design to craft sensory experiences. With over 8 years of teaching, she’s guided hundreds in turning wax into art.

This Workshop Has Ended

Thank you to all the participants who joined us and made this workshop a success! Stay tuned for more such cultural experiences in the coming months.

Location: Nanital, Uttarakhand

Dates: 7 June 2025

Time: 10 AM – 3 PM
