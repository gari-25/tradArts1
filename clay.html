From clay to creation

Step into the calming world of clay and creativity. "Clay & Soul" is a hands-on pottery workshop where you’ll discover the therapeutic joy of molding clay with your own hands. Whether you’re a beginner or just curious, learn wheel-throwing and hand-building techniques in a friendly, earthy atmosphere. Let the clay tell your story.

Your Workshop Guide

Rakesh Patel

A passionate ceramic artist from Gujarat, Rakesh Patel brings over 10+ years of experience in traditional and contemporary pottery. Known for blending rural aesthetics with modern styles, Rakesh has exhibited across India and believes that working with clay is not just art — it's a soulful meditation. With his gentle guidance and lively energy, every participant walks away not just with a pot, but with peace.
