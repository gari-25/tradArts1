What is Madhubani Art?

Rooted in the heart of Bihar, Madhubani or Mithila art is a tapestry of tradition and color. With motifs of flora, fauna, and folklore, this art is passed from mother to daughter, not just as a skill but as a soul language of celebration and ritual.

The town of Madhubani, close to the Nepal border, is the epicenter of this craft — where walls, floors, and even utensils tell tales with every stroke.
