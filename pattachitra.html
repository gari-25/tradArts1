Pattachitra – A Journey in Strokes and Stories

Our Pattachitra Art Workshop brought to life one of India’s oldest storytelling traditions, where every stroke echoes folklore and devotion. Participants immersed themselves in the graceful intricacies of natural dyes, mythological themes, and iconic motifs that define this heritage art.

Guided by a seasoned artisan, the session was a serene dive into disciplined brushwork, traditional canvas preparation, and the soulful philosophy behind each design. The air was filled with the scent of tamarind paste and colors extracted from nature — just like the age-old practice.

From curious beginners to passionate art lovers, everyone left not just with their own masterpiece, but with a deeper respect for the legacy carried in every Pattachitra.

Workshop Instructor

Bijay Mohapatra

A master Pattachitra artist from Raghurajpur, Odisha — Bijay ji carries a lineage of traditional storytelling through his brush. With decades of experience, his calm presence and deep knowledge made the session both educational and spiritually enriching.
