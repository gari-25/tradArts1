Rangoli Radiance

The Rangoli Radiance workshop brought a burst of colors and creativity to life as participants explored the traditional art of Rangoli. Celebrating India’s rich festive culture, the event transformed the space with intricate designs crafted using colored powders, petals, and natural elements. Attendees experienced how this timeless art form welcomes joy, harmony, and spiritual positivity into our surroundings.

Through guided practice, everyone created beautiful Rangoli pieces blending classic motifs with modern freestyle expressions.

Workshop Instructor

Ramana Shah

A folk art revivalist and cultural educator who has conducted 50+ workshops across India.
