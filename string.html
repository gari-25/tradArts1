String Saga

String Saga is a mesmerizing kathputli-inspired exhibition that brings the ancient art of puppetry to life through vibrant threads and handcrafted wooden marvels. Rooted in the rich folk traditions of Rajasthan and beyond, this saga invites you to experience stories told not by voices, but by the rhythmic movements of strings and silhouettes. From royal tales to rural legends, each puppet unravels a piece of India’s cultural soul—dancing, swaying, and expressing emotion in its most vivid form. Whether it’s the clack of wooden feet or the flutter of a puppet’s dupatta, String Saga is a tribute to an age-old storytelling tradition, celebrating artistry that speaks beyond words.
