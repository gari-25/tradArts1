Threads of timeless tradition

Unfold the beauty of fabric as a canvas! This creative workshop introduces you to the playful world of fabric painting, printing, and dyeing — perfect for those who love colors, textures, and hands-on expression.

Your Workshop Guide

Neha Sharma

From dyeing to weaving, Neha brings the art of fabric-making alive with hands-on techniques and regional flair. Passionate about preserving India’s textile heritage, she guides you through the magic of fabric transformation using natural colors and traditional motifs.
