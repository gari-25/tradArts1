Frequently Asked Questions

What is Kalakosh?

Kalakosh, meaning "Treasure of Art," is a digital platform dedicated to showcasing the diverse traditional art forms of India. We aim to preserve, promote, and educate about India's rich artistic heritage.

How can I explore the art forms on your website?

You can explore art forms by various categories, including regions, and through our comprehensive gallery. Each section provides detailed information and visuals about unique art pieces and traditions.

Can I purchase art through Kalakosh?

Currently, Kalakosh is an informational and educational platform. We do not facilitate direct sales. However, we may provide links to artisans or organizations where you can acquire authentic pieces in the future.

Is the content on Kalakosh copyrighted?

All original content, text, images, and design on Kalakosh are protected by copyright laws. While you are welcome to browse and learn, reproduction or distribution of our content without explicit permission is prohibited. Please refer to our Terms of Service for more details.

How can I contribute to Kalakosh?

We welcome contributions from artists, researchers, and art enthusiasts! If you have unique insights, high-quality images, or would like to collaborate, please reach out to us via our Contact Us page. We're always looking to expand our treasure trove of Indian art.

What are your future plans for Kalakosh?

We are continuously working to enhance Kalakosh! Our future plans include expanding our art form database, adding educational resources like artist interviews and technique guides, and potentially integrating features for direct artist support or online workshops.
