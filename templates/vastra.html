Vastra Vismaya

VastraVismay is a vibrant celebration of India's timeless handloom heritage — an exhibition that brings together the rich tapestry of regional sarees from across the nation. From the intricate Chikankari of Lucknow to the regal Banarasi silks, from the breezy Kerala Kasavu to the colorful Bandhani of Gujarat — every weave here tells a story, woven with tradition, culture, and the magic of skilled artisans. Step into a space where threads meet tales, and experience the soul of India draped in elegance.
