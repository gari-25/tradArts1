Warli Storytelling in Symbols

Dive into the world of tribal tales told without words. This workshop explores Warli art, a traditional form from Maharashtra, where simple white symbols on earthy backgrounds narrate stories of community, nature, and daily life. You'll learn how to create your own visual story using this ancient symbolic language.

Your Workshop Guide

Sunita Rao

A seasoned Warli artist from Palghar, Sunita has been practicing and teaching this art for over a decade. Her work has been featured in cultural exhibitions across India, and her passion lies in preserving the spirit of Warli while making it accessible to all.

Dates: 28 July 2025
